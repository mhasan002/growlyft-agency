For the Services page :
## **1️⃣ Let’s Talk (top hero button)**

When clicked:

* Opens a **pop-up form** (modal) or takes them to the **Contact Page form** directly.
* Fields (all required except message):

  1. Full Name
  2. Business Name
  3. Website or Social Media Link
  4. Contact Email
  5.Phone number(country code add)
  6. Minimum Budget
  7. Message (optional)
* After submission → Success message:

  > ✅ Thanks for reaching out! We’ll review your details and get back to you within 24 hours.

---
